# Essential Knowledge Instructions

## Purpose
Help distributors and sales staff understand what this product is, what it does, and why they should recommend it to customers. Viewers should feel confident explaining the product's main purpose and benefit to others.

## Target Audience
- Sales reps and distributors new to this product
- Sales staff briefing customers
- Anyone learning about M.L. Campbell product offerings

## Content Structure

### 1. **Safety Grabber (15-30 seconds)**
- Brief statement about safe handling
- Sets professional tone
- Example: "This is an industrial wood coating. Proper handling is essential."

### 2. **Introduction (30-45 seconds)**
- Product name
- Simple one-sentence description of what it is
- Example: "ARROYO 1K Waterborne Clear Topcoat is a protective coating for wood surfaces"

### 3. **What Problem Does It Solve? (1-1.5 minutes)**
- Describe a common customer need or problem
- Explain how this product solves it
- Use real-world scenarios
- Example: "Customers need wood protection that dries fast. This product does that."

### 4. **What Makes It Different? (1-1.5 minutes)**
- 2-3 key features explained simply
- Why these matter to the customer
- Real-world benefits
- Example: "It dries in hours, not days. That means faster job completion."

### 5. **Who Uses It & Where? (30-45 seconds)**
- Typical customers or industries
- Real-world applications
- Where you'd see this product being used

### 6. **Safety Reminder (15-30 seconds)**
- One key safety point
- Direct to SDS for details
- Example: "Remember, proper ventilation during application is essential. Always check the SDS."

## Key Guidelines
- **Keep it simple**: Sales staff should understand the basics
- **No jargon**: Avoid technical terms or explain clearly
- **Focus on customer benefits**: "Saves customer time" not "fast dry time"
- **Visual support**: Simple diagrams or product shots help
- **Professional tone**: Confident, clear, helpful
- **Maximum 5 minutes**: Hold attention of busy professionals
- **Sourced information**: Everything from website, sell sheet, or PDS

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ✗ Too much technical detail
- ✗ Assuming the viewer knows what "waterborne" or "topcoat" means
- ✗ Listing features without explaining benefits
- ✗ Speaking too fast
- ✗ Boring, monotone delivery

## Example Opening
"Welcome. This video is about ARROYO Waterborne Clear Topcoat. If you're in sales, you'll want to know what this product does and why your customers might need it. Let's start by looking at a real-world example."
